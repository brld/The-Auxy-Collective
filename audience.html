Our Network

Below is our audience of labels and artists that enable us to give our artists
within the Auxy Community the recognition they deserve.

1 / 5
LUNCHBOX
LUNCHBOX is a label that specializes in modern genres such as liquid funk and chill / ambient tunes. They are one of The Auxy Collective's first supporters and continue to consistently support our artists and help extend the audience that they can reach.
2 / 5
EDM Central
EDM Central has been with us from the very beginning and focused on reposting and releasing future bass, melodic dubstep, chill trap, certain variants of house, and ambient tracks. Make sure to check them out and give them some love on behalf of AC.
3 / 5
The Music Spotlight
Run by one of our very own, The Music Spotlight will repost and support most genres on request, though it does not do releases on its page. It was established in mid 2017 and has been going strong since its founding by Hum4n01d.
4 / 5
BONSAI Collective
BONSAI Collective was established in 2018 by Capshun and has been rapidly expanding since its founding. It's co-curated by paaus and is with the Phuture collective family as being associated with the LUNCHBOX label.
5 / 5
Phuture Collective
Phuture Collective is our largest supporter label and is part of the Auxy Collective network with two of it's sublabels PIL Collective and TYDE. It's run by Michael and has a thriving community of music producers and label owners.
