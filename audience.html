Our Network

Below is our audience of labels and artists that enable us to give our artists within the Auxy Community the recognition they deserve.

Phuture Collective

Phuture Collective is our largest supporter label and is part of the Auxy Collective network with its sublabels PIL Society and TYDE. It's run by Michael and has a thriving community of music producers and label owners.

LUNCH BOX

LUNCH BOX is a label that specializes in modern genres, such as liquid funk and chill / ambient tunes. They are one of The Auxy Collective's first supporters, and continue to consistently support our artists and help extend the audience that they can reach.

Ethereal Sound

Ethereal Sound has been with us from the very beginning, and is focused on releasing and reposting future bass, melodic dubstep, chill trap, certain variants of house, and ambient tracks. Make sure to check them out and give them some love on behalf of AC.

BONSAI Collective

BONSAI Collective was established in 2018 by Capshun and has been rapidly expanding since its founding. It's co-curated by paaus and is in the Phuture Collective family, associated with the LUNCH BOX label.

The Music Spotlight

Run by one of our very own, The Music Spotlight will repost and support most genres on request, though it does not release on its page. It was established in mid-2017 and has been going strong since its founding by Hum4n01d.
